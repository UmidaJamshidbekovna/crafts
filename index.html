ARTISANS ARE LISTED IN....

All artisans presenting in the platform are heritage driven and majority of them represent dynasties of craftmakers producing unique products, with a deep knowledge on local craft, mastering craft techniques that are at risk of disappearing, using environmentally-friendly materials. These artisans are also socially innovative, in the sense that they work with marginalized communities, support female work, and create work for those that might be left behind by institutions.

FULL PROFILE

Odilbek Matchanov is a leading master of the Madir School of Ceramics, named after a village in Khorezm region (north-western part of Uzbekistan). He comes from the famous potters dynasty, but earned a wide recognition on his own turn as a strict follower of authentic principles of the Madir school.
